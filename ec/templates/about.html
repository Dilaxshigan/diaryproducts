About Us


Fresh Milk Products, based in Ambewala, Sri Lanka, is a trusted local dairy company known for its high-quality offerings, including milk, yogurt, and cheese. With a commitment to sourcing milk from local farmers, the company ensures both freshness and a positive impact on the community's economy. Renowned for stringent quality control and hygiene, Fresh Milk Products stands out as the preferred choice for premium dairy products in Ambewala and its surrounding areas.



Mission


"At Fresh Milk Products, our mission is to provide Ambewala, Sri Lanka, and beyond with premium-quality dairy offerings sourced locally. We prioritize freshness, sustainability, and community well-being through strong relationships with local farmers. Committed to stringent quality standards, we aim to be the preferred choice for wholesome and delicious dairy products, emphasizing innovation and customer satisfaction."
